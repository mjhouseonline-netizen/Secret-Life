Privacy Policy

The Secret Life Of Your Pet • Studio Edition

Last Updated: March 20, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to The Secret Life Of Your Pet (the "App"). We prioritize the privacy of our creators. This policy outlines how your data is handled within our AI-powered production environment.

2. Google OAuth & Scopes

Our App integrates with Google Identity Services to provide seamless login and cloud backup. We request the following permissions:

  • Email & Profile: Used strictly for account identification and credit management.
  • Google Drive (drive.file): Used only to save your generated productions to a private folder. We cannot access, read, or delete any files in your Drive that were not created by this App.

3. AI Data Processing

Content generation is powered by Google Gemini and Veo.

  • Images and audio uploaded for "Style DNA" or "Character Profiles" are processed transiently to generate your requests.
  • Data is transmitted securely to the Gemini API and is subject to Google's Enterprise Privacy commitments.
  • We do not store your uploaded media on our private servers; everything is kept in your local browser storage or your authorized Google Drive.

4. Content Safety

The App enforces a "Mobile Safe Mode" which uses AI-driven filters to ensure all generated content is PG-rated and family-friendly.
